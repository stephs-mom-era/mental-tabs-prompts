5 AI Prompts to Close
Your Mental Tabs

For the working mom whose brain doesn't have an off switch.

Made by Steph · working mom · simple systems to help you reduce the mental load
1Open ChatGPT or Claude — both are free.
2Copy a prompt below and paste it into the chat.
3Add your situation at the end. Hit send.
💡 Skip the typing entirely

Use the microphone on your phone keyboard to talk instead of type. AI is smart enough to understand even messy talk-to-text. Way faster.

How to Keep These Handy

✦ Easiest option

Bookmark this page and come back whenever you need it. Done.

✦ Want them inside your AI app?

Hit "Copy All Prompts" below. Paste into a new chat and say: "These are my go-to prompts — save them so I can come back to them." Bookmark that chat.

The Prompts
Prompt 1
The Parenting Guru

I need practical parenting advice I can actually use today. Use evidence-based gentle parenting and child development principles, but keep it realistic — I'm not looking for perfect, I'm looking for something that might actually work tonight. Give me 2 or 3 things I can try right now. Here's what's happening:

👉 Speak or type the specific behavior or situation — the more specific, the better the advice.
Prompt 2
The Dinner Decider

I need dinner ideas for tonight. I have picky kids and I'm tired. Give me 3 options that take 30 minutes or less, use normal ingredients, and have a realistic shot at actually getting eaten. Here's what I'm working with:

👉 Speak or type what's in your fridge and pantry — or snap a photo and attach it before you hit send.
Prompt 3
The Brain Dump Clearer

I'm a working mom and my brain is completely overwhelmed right now. I'm going to give you everything that's swirling in my head — tasks I need to do, things I'm worried about, stuff I promised people, appointments I might be forgetting, and random thoughts taking up space. Sort all of it into three buckets: what genuinely needs to happen today, what can realistically wait, and what I should just let go of entirely. Be honest and be ruthless. Here's everything:

👉 Speak or type your chaos here — don't filter it, just dump everything out. Then hit send.
Prompt 4
The Morning Brief

I need a quick rundown before I leave the house. Look at my day and tell me: what do I need to prep right now, what am I probably forgetting, and is there anything I should handle before things get chaotic. Keep it fast and practical. Here's my day:

👉 Speak or type your calendar and anything you know is coming today.
Prompt 5
The Gentle Reframe

I'm having a hard mom moment and I need you to talk me off the ledge like a smart, kind friend — not a therapist, not a motivational poster, just someone who gets it. Acknowledge what I'm feeling first, then help me see it differently if you can. Here's what's going on:

👉 Speak or type what happened — or just how you're feeling. You don't have to explain it perfectly.
🎁 Bonus Prompt
The Snack Scramble

I need a balanced snack idea for my kids right now — something with a protein, a fruit, and a carb. Quick, no cooking, not too unhealthy, and ideally something that won't cause a meltdown. Give me 3 options using what I have on hand. Here's what's available:

👉 Speak or type what you have — or snap a photo of your fridge or pantry and attach it before you hit send.
